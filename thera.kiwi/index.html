Why TheraKiwi

Online therapeutic consultations are rapidly developing. In therapies that focus exclusively on talking, a video conference is sufficient. In other forms of support, tools are used (images, figurines...) on a surface where therapist and client interact. How to allow this richness online?

TheraKiwi answers this need, by allowing the therapist and the client to increase their interaction capacities through a shared visual space, centered on the notion of cards.

With TheraKiwi, the online therapeutic space comes to life and opens up to analogical communication, the creative dimension, imagination, the symbolic, reverie, the metaphor and play.

How it works
Create a meeting with a client

Create or login to your account, create a new meeting and send a link to it to the client, it's simple. Is one session not enough? You can save your session and return later to where you left off.

Select an available or upload your own deck of cards

Choose a ready-made collection or create your own from already downloaded cards or if you have physical cards that you have been working with for a long time, you can upload them to our digital environment and continue working comfortably

Сonfigure and adapt the interactive sequence and options at any time

Display a deck of cards face down or face up, allow the client to scroll through the cards, to choose one or more of them, to arrange them in his or her private space, to turn them over, to annotate them or to place them in the shared space
